Scientists around the world want to find out. The
plastic “end-of-life challenge” calls for new ways
to recycle and reuse plastics endlessly in a
closed loop system, so they never become
waste. Innovation on that scale would convert
the current “make-take-dispose” linear economy
into a circular economy, where recycling plastic
for eternity is possible.
